Mangú Con Los Tres Golpes Recipe

Description

This traditional Dominican recipe for Mangu Con Los Tres Golpes is a tasty dish that is a mix of many different foods and flavors all put onto one plate! The dish consists of plantain, salami, cheese, and eggs.

Ingredients

  • Green Plantains
  • Eggs
  • Queso de Freir (fried cheese)
  • Salami
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Peel the green plantains and cut them into small pieces, and then put them into a bowl of boiling salted water.
  2. Cut pieces of salami and cheese, and crack open however many eggs you'd like. Put all three of these in a pan to fry.
  3. Remove the plantain when they are tender and a deep yellow color. Put them into plate, and mash them together with butter. This is mangú.
  4. Remove the eggs, cheese, and salami when they're done frying and place them in the same plate as the mangú.
