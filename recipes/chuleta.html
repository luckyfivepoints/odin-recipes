Chuletas Fritas Recipe

Description

This traditional Puerto Rican recipe for Chuletas Fritas (Fried Pork) is a delicious recipe that tastes like no other fried pork chops that you have tried before! This recipe uses a plethora of seasonings to bring a flavorful and distinct taste to the pork.

Ingredients

  • Pork Chops (bone-in or boneless is fine)
  • Adobo Seasoning Mix
  • Sazon
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the pork chops with the Adobo seasoning mix, a Sazon packet, onion powder, along with some salt and papper. Mix together the pork chops with the seasoning and put the pork chops in a bowl inside the fridge so it can marinate.
  2. Remove the pork chops after a few hours or so, and put them in a pan to fry. Make sure to flip to both sides of the pork schops.
  3. Take out the pork chops from the pan when they are nice and crisp.
